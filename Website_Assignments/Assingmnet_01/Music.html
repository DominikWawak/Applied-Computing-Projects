I feel music is something that no matter what person in the world you are you can still find something that you like or can relate to.

My parents made me start music back at the age of 7 when a random flyer came to our post box. I started out playing guitar and piano, later on I quit my piano lessons and stuck with the guitar only. Throughout my secondary school years I made two rock bands but didn't really have much sucess with both. Having said that its still one of my favourite hobbies that I enjoy doing in my spare time.
