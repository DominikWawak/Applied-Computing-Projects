I liked skateboarding from a very young age. To put it out there Im not really good, to tell the truth Im pretty trash at it but that doesnt mean I don't enjoy it. Watching skateboarders preform tricks its something out of this world. I always think they must have superpowers and absolutely 0% fear. Skateboarding helps you challenge your fears and gives you this sweet adrenaline rush that keeps you going. My favourite skateboarding channel is Braille , you should check them out , they are crazy.
